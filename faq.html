Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about using Agri1.

General

What is Agri1?
Agri1 is a free, AI-powered farming assistant platform that provides live mandi prices, weather forecasts, crop guidance, pest management solutions, and government scheme information — all designed specifically for Indian farmers.
Is Agri1 free to use?
Yes! Agri1 is completely free. All features — including the AI chatbot, live market prices, and weather forecasts — are available at no cost.
Who created Agri1?
Agri1 was created by Satyam Kumar, a passionate developer based at Lovely Professional University (LPU), India. He designed and built every component of the platform.
Does Agri1 work on mobile phones?
Absolutely. Agri1 is fully responsive and optimized for all devices — smartphones, tablets, and desktops. No app download is needed; just open it in your browser.

AI Assistant

What can the AI assistant help with?
The AI assistant can answer questions about crop cultivation, mandi prices, pest and disease management, weather conditions, government farming schemes, fertilizers, irrigation, and more. It supports both English and Hindi.
Can I ask non-farming questions?
No. The Agri1 AI is fine-tuned exclusively for agriculture and farming topics. It will politely decline any questions unrelated to farming.
Does it understand Hindi?
Yes! You can type in Romanized Hindi (e.g., "Gehu ka bhav batao" or "Tamatar kitna hai"). The AI understands 30+ crop names in Hindi along with common farming terms.

Market Prices

Where does the price data come from?
Live mandi prices are fetched from the official data.gov.in (Agmarknet) API, which provides government-verified commodity prices from mandis across India.
How often are prices updated?
Prices are fetched in real-time from the API. The platform caches data for 5 minutes to ensure fast loading. The API itself updates prices as mandis report them daily.
Can I see the nearest mandi?
Yes. When you allow location access, Agri1 automatically sorts results by distance, showing the nearest mandis first with a distance badge and highlighting the closest one.

Weather

How accurate are the weather forecasts?
Weather data is powered by the Open-Meteo API, which aggregates data from multiple meteorological sources. Forecasts are generally accurate but may vary for hyper-local conditions.
What does rain probability mean?
Rain probability shows the maximum chance of precipitation on a given day. For example, 70% means there is a high likelihood of rainfall. Use this to plan your spraying, harvesting, and irrigation schedule.

Account & Privacy

Do I need to create an account?
No account is required to use most features. You can browse prices, weather, and the AI assistant without signing up. An account lets you save preferences and track your farming data.
Is my data safe?
Agri1 stores minimal data locally on your device (using browser storage). We do not collect personal information, track your activity, or share data with third parties.

Still have questions?

Our AI assistant is available 24/7 to help you with any farming-related queries.

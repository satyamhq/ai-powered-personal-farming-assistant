Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about using Agri1

Is Agri1 free for farmers?

Yes, Agri1 is completely free to use for all farmers. You can access weather, market prices, and AI advice without any cost.

How accurate is the weather forecast?

We use advanced meteorological data from open-source satellite APIs (Open-Meteo) to provide accurate location-based forecasts updated every hour.

Where do you get Mandi prices?

We fetch real-time data from the government's Agmarknet portal via APIs. Prices are updated daily as mandis upload them.

How can the AI Assistant help me?

The AI Assistant can answer questions about crop diseases, pest control, fertilizer dosage, best sowing times, and government schemes in simple language.

Can I change the app language?

Yes! You can use the Google Translate widget at the bottom of the page or change your preference in the Account settings.

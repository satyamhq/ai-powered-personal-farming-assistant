About Agri1

India's AI-powered farming assistant — making smart agriculture accessible to every farmer through real-time data and intelligent technology.

Our Mission

Agri1 was created with a single goal — to bridge the gap between technology and Indian agriculture. Millions of farmers lack access to real-time market prices, accurate weather forecasts, and expert crop guidance. Agri1 brings all of this together in one simple, mobile-friendly platform powered by AI.

Live Market Prices

Real-time mandi rates from 300+ markets across India, sorted by nearest location with price comparison.

AI Crop Assistant

A fine-tuned farming-only chatbot that understands English and Hindi, with live price and weather integration.

7-Day Weather Forecast

Rain probability, temperature trends, and AI-generated farming advisories tailored to your location.

Pest & Disease Solutions

Identification guides and treatment plans for common crop pests with visual references.

Our Technology

Agri1 is built entirely with vanilla HTML, CSS, and JavaScript — no heavy frameworks — ensuring fast load times even on low-bandwidth rural connections. It integrates with the data.gov.in Agmarknet API for live mandi prices, the Open-Meteo API for weather forecasts, and Nominatim for location services. The AI assistant uses a custom intent-detection engine with bilingual NLP support for English and Hindi.

Why Agri1?

Farming is the backbone of India. Yet most farmers depend on word-of-mouth for market prices, guesswork for weather planning, and trial-and-error for pest management. Agri1 changes that by putting verified, real-time data directly in the hands of those who need it most — for free, in their language, on any device.

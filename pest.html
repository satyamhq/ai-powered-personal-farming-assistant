Common Crop Pests & Remedies

Aphids

Small soft-bodied insects that suck nutrient-rich liquids from plants. Causes yellowing and distorted leaves.

Spider Mites

Tiny reddish or brown arachnids. Look for fine silk webbing on the underside of leaves and stippling.

Fall Armyworm

Voracious caterpillars that feed on maize and other crops. Look for ragged holes in leaves and sawdust-like frass.

Root Knot Nematodes

Microscopic worms that cause galls or knots on roots. Results in stunted growth and yellowing.

Stem Borer

These pests attack plant roots and form knots. Plants become weak and stunted. Crop rotation and resistant varieties are the best prevention

Leaf-Miners

Leaf miners create white zig-zag lines inside leaves and weaken plants. Early neem spray helps control them.

Bollworm

Bollworm is a harmful caterpillar that bores into fruits and bolls, causing heavy crop damage. Early control is essential.

Whiteflies

Whiteflies are tiny white insects that suck plant sap and spread diseases. They weaken crops and reduce yield quickly.

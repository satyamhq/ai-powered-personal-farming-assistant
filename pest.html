Common Crop Pests & Remedies

Aphids

Small soft-bodied insects sucking sap. Causes yellowing and distorted leaves.

Fall Armyworm

Voracious caterpillars feeding on maize. Look for ragged holes and sawdust frass.

Whiteflies

Tiny white flying insects. Transmit viruses and cause sooty mold.

Stem Borer

Larvae bore into stems causing 'dead heart' in rice and drying of central shoot.

Thrips

Tiny insects causing curling of leaves and silver/bronze streaks.

Mealybugs

White cottony masses on stems/leaves. Suck sap and weaken plants.

Bollworm

Larvae bore into cotton bolls, destroying lint and seeds.

Cutworms

Caterpillars that cut down young seedlings at ground level during the night.

Leaf Miner

Larvae tunnel inside leaves creating white zigzag lines or blisters.

Grasshoppers

Chewing insects that eat foliage, stems, and flowers. Can come in swarms.

Red Spider Mites

Tiny reddish mites causing yellowing, stippling, and fine webbing.

Termites

Social insects that feed on roots and stems, causing wilting and drying.
